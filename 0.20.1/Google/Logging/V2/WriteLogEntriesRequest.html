RepeatedField getEntries()

Required. The log entries to write. Values supplied for the fields log_name, resource, and labels in this entries.write request are inserted into those log entries in this list that do not provide their own values.

Stackdriver Logging also creates and inserts values for timestamp and insert_id if the entries do not provide them. The created insert_id for the N'th entry in this list will be greater than earlier entries and less than later entries. Otherwise, the order of log entries in this list does not matter. To improve throughput and to avoid exceeding the quota limit for calls to entries.write, you should write multiple log entries at once rather than calling this method for each individual log entry.
