FaceAnnotation_Landmark_Type

class FaceAnnotation_Landmark_Type

Face landmark (feature) type.

Left and right are defined from the vantage of the viewer of the image without considering mirror projections typical of photos. So, LEFT_EYE, typically, is the person's right eye.
